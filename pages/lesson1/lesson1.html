Picking the Right Entity for Your Silicon Beach Startup

When deciding what type of entity to form to create you new, awesome, soon to make you millions of dollars startup company, you should consider the advantages and disadvantages of each type. In California, the following entities are the most common:

  1. C Corporation
  2. S Corporation
  3. LLC

Not legal advice. Speak to a business lawyer to determine which entity is best for your particular situation.
